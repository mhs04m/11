ADMISSION PROCESS INTO PHD:

IIIT Naya Raipur invites applications for admission to the M.S. (R) and Ph.D. Programs in all disciplines of Engineering, Science, English, and Management. The goal of these programs at IIIT-NR is to prepare the scholars for becoming global academic, industrial and R&D leaders.

Scholars admitted to Ph.D. program under the regular Institute Scholar scheme (full time) are eligible for the Institute Teaching/Research Assistantship of Rs 31000/- per month, as per MHRD guidelines.

Students are encouraged to present research papers at reputed conferences and for which the institute supports financially as per the norms of IIIT Naya Raipur.

Applicants must read Information Brochure for M.S. (R) & Ph.D. Admission of the institute for eligibility criteria, Admission category, Research areas, and other details

Please read the instructions given below before filling up the application form.

All applications will be received through the online portal of IIIT Naya Raipur only. An applicant has to register online by filling in the required details. The login credentials will be provided to the applicant on an email used for registration.

The applicant has to log in with details received and complete the application by providing all the necessary information. Please note that the applicant has to upload the testimonials wherever it is required to validate the information along with the photograph and signature.

After completion of filling the application, the applicant will be directed to the payment gateway. Once the payment is done successfully, the applicant will be asked to confirm the final submission of the application.

After the final submission, an acknowledgment of payment along with a copy of the application will be provided for future reference. The applicant has to carry the printout of this application along with all the attachments and their originals during the written test/interview.

The applicant has to register separately (the same email id can be used) to apply for multiple disciplines and payment is also to be made separately.
