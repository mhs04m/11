Why Join IIITNR?

INNOVATIVE ACADEMICS

IIIT-NR academics has several innovative features, which differentiates it from other similar institutes and helps students acquire skills necessary for their profession. Some of the salient features are:

  • Skill First, Theory Later, Projects Integrated model for sound foundation of fundamentals
  • Fractal Academics with short modular courses to enable inclusion of latest technologies in course curriculum
  • 'T'-Education model to enable students to acquire both breadth and depth of knowledge
  • Elective subjects and multidisciplinary project options encourage students to develop interdisciplinary skills
  • Course curriculum judiciously designed to offer balanced exposure to both Hardware and Software with diverse set of tools
  • Strong Industry-linkages for internships and industry-elective subjects in course curriculum
