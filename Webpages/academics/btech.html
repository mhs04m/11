    DSPM-IIITNR currently runs three undergraduate programs: B.Tech. in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) , B.Tech. in Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE) and B.Tech. in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (DSAI) . These credit-based programs are designed to foster core skills with innovation and entrepreneurship skills among students. The dynamic structure of the curriculum orchestrates with the changing needs of industry and academia. IIIT Naya Raipur follows the Fractal Curriculum
